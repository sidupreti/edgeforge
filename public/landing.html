How it works

Six steps from idea to firmware

01 / 06

Setup

Describe your application, pick your sensor and MCU. The AI onboarding detects your data format automatically.

02 / 06

Collect

Upload labelled CSV files or stream live from your device over Serial, BLE, or Wi-Fi MQTT.

03 / 06

Pipeline

Configure filtering, normalization, and feature extraction. The AI Pipeline Designer recommends optimal settings for your signal type.

04 / 06

Train

One click trains Random Forest, SVM, and Neural Network classifiers simultaneously. Cross-validated accuracy shown for each.

05 / 06

Validate

Upload test CSV files or stream live from hardware. Confidence scores and per-class probabilities update in real time.

06 / 06

Export

Download a classifier.h C header, Python session script, or EdgeForge package — ready to integrate into your firmware.
